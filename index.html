About Me

I’m Sammy (Yung-Sian), a Procurement Operations Analyst at HPE, where I specialize in the strategic procurement of high-value components and managing complex supply chains. My role is about balancing cost, quality, and timely delivery to drive operational excellence.


My journey started with a solid foundation in Computer Science from National Dong Hwa University. I didn’t just stick to the books—I applied my learning to real-world challenges, particularly in cryptography, cybersecurity, deep learning, and computer vision. My work in these areas has led to published research and innovative solutions that have been showcased at international forums.


Before diving into procurement, I gained valuable experience as a Software Engineer in HPE's Global Operations Engineering department, where I coordinated supply chain activities and ensured seamless project execution. An internship in R&D, focusing on security hardware modules, further sharpened my ability to manage projects and supply chains with precision.


Beyond the technical, I thrive in collaborative environments. My adaptability and leadership have been key in working with diverse teams across various disciplines. I’m also an active member of tech communities like Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) and GDG Cloud Taipei, where I enjoy sharing knowledge and making complex ideas accessible.


My career is driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to fostering inclusive, innovative tech spaces. I’m always eager to connect, collaborate, and contribute to the ongoing dialogue of technological advancement.
